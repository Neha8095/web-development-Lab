Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is a technology used to convert handwritten and printed text from images into editable and searchable digital text.

Why OCR is Important

  • Reduces manual data entry
  • Saves time and effort
  • Improves document digitization

Applications of OCR

  1. Education – digitizing notes and answer sheets
  2. Banking – cheque and form processing
  3. Offices – document archiving

Process of OCR

Image Capture → Pre-processing → Text Detection → Character Recognition → Output Generation

Conclusion

OCR is a trending technology that plays an important role in digital transformation.
